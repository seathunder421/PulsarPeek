PulsarPeek 🌀

A Desktop Tool for Pulsar Data Browsing & Visualization

Built on the ATNF Pulsar Catalogue — explore pulsar parameters, distributions, and associations across categories.

Category Filtering

Quickly filter pulsars by category and compare parameter characteristics across different types.

Single-Source Detail Viewer

Click any pulsar to view full parameter details, including period, DM, magnetic field strength, and more.

P-Ṗ Diagram & Sky Map

Interactive P-Ṗ diagram and celestial sphere map for pulsar population analysis and spatial distribution.

One-click CSV export of filtered results for further analysis and paper citations.
